How to use
1

Paste full data

Copy your full wind sector dataset (CSV or tab-separated). Column 8 must contain energy in GWh. Any row whose text includes "sector" is treated as a header and skipped.

2

Add curtailment scenarios

Add one or more scenarios. Paste the curtailed dataset and enter the turbulence sector range (e.g. 51-92). Wrap-around ranges like 350-20 are supported.

3

Calculate

Click Calculate losses. The tool computes sector-by-sector overlap fractions and energy losses in GWh and kWh, then shows combined totals across all scenarios.

4

Export

Copy results as tab-separated values for Excel, or download a formatted .xlsx file with one sheet per scenario plus a summary sheet.
